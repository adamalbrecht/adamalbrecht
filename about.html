About

Adam Albrecht is an independent software developer currently living and working in San Francisco. After graduating from college at Miami University, Adam worked as a consultant for Sogeti in Columbus, Ohio for several years before venturing out on his own. He has since done client work for companies such as Hubspot, Alliance Data Systems, Dynamit, M/I Homes, and several others. He also caught the startup bug and developed a SAAS system for healthcare organizations called PrivacyPro. And he's currently building another product in the legal space called NextChapter.

Adam's main areas of expertise include Ruby on Rails, thick-client Javascript apps using Angular or Backbone, and front-end web in general. He stresses customer-friendly user experiences and test-driven development. Outside of work, Adam has a passion for travel, photography, fitness, and his beloved Cincinnati Reds and Ohio State Buckeyes.
